Folk-pop
The performers use traditional Croatian tattoos known as sikanje. They were used to mark a child as Christian, so that they could not be kidnapped and converted to Islam.
In Turkey, the song and music video caused a scandal: the lyrics and visuals were seen as criticism of the Ottoman Empire and as a depiction of Croatian women being enslaved by Turks.
