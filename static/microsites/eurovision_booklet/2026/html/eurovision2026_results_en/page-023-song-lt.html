At the end of the song, Lion sings “I just want more” in five languages: Spanish, German, French, Italian, and Lithuanian.
In French, “Je veux juste plus” can mean both “I just want more” and “I just don’t want more,” depending on whether the “s” in “plus” is pronounced. In the studio recording, the singer pronounces the “s,” but at Eurovision he corrected the mistake and stopped pronouncing it.
The song ends with Lion touching his hands together 2–3 times while forming an “O” shape with them; in International Sign, this means the word “more.”
