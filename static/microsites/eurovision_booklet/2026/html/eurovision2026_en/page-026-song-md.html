The song mentions many Moldovan cultural references: the city of Soroca, the song genre doina, the hora dance, the Soviet film Maria, Mirabela, the song “Focul Din Vatră”, composer Eugen Doga, poet Grigore Vieru, and more.
One of the backing vocalists, Aliona Moon, has already been to Eurovision twice: in 2012, also as a backing vocalist, and in 2013 with a solo performance.
